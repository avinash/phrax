How to Use Phr@X
 
Zoom In & Zoom Out
  • To zoom in, Left Click.
  • To zoom out, Right Click.

Open

Click this button to open a fractal. You may only load XML (Extensible Markup Language) files in Phr@X.

Select Type

When the application loads, you are provided with an initial mandelbrot fractal. To view other fractals, go to Fractals > Select Type. Here you'll find 10 fractals, 5 from the Mandelbrot Set & 5 from the Julia Set.

Save & Save As

These buttons save your fractal in the XML format. These files can later be loaded again in Phr@X.

Save Image

Use this option to save your fractal in either the JPEG or the PNG format. With these formats you may view the fractals with a software that support these formats.

Reset

Click this button to reset the fractal to the initial state.

Help Quick Guide

Phr@X Quick Guide helps the new users with a quick help on how to zoom in and zoom out on the fractals.

What's This?

Click on this button and then click on a button to know what that button does.
