Welcome to Phr@X
 

Phr@X is a free software, developed to generate and explore fractals. If you are new to fractals, please consult our What are fractals section.

Phr@X has been developped by Imtiaz Ellaheebuksh and another student. We are two students at the University of Mauritus and we have developed Phr@X as our Final Year Project.
